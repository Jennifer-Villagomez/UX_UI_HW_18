Let’s build experiences that help users feel heard.

Hello, I am Jennifer Villagomez, a UX researcher & designer. I specialize in user research, ideation, prototyping and usability testing. I enjoy solving user problems that create more thoughtful and inclusive experiences to benefit users. My skills include Figma, Miro, and Invision. I approach my work as a designer and researcher with curiosity, empathy and advocacy on behalf of the users.

My UX Skills & Methods

User Research & Interview Planning

I’m an analytical person who enjoys putting together open ended questions and putting together research objectives that will lead me to uncover hidden problems with tangible solutions.

Ideation & Brainstorming

I enjoy taking my data synthesized from interviews and looking for patterns in the Affinity Diagrams that help me create believable Personas. Using Miro, I write down my ideas from simple to futristic and make sense of them via Feature Prioritization Matrix.

Scenario Design & Storytelling

As I identify the problems and insights of users, I prefer to really immerse myself in the movements of users by creating scenarios and journey mapping in order to understand the painpoints they might encounter while moving through a product

Wireframing, Prototyping, & Usability Testing

Wireframing is where I see my ideas come to life. By using mid and lo-fi prototypes, I’m able to spot visibility issues before I get too far along in the visual design process and still gain enlightening insights into how users move through the product, instead of just how I expect them to move through it.
